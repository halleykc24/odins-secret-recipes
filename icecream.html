Thors Thunder Ice Cream

Thor has long since hung up his hammer. His days of war now over he spends his time baking, cooking, frying, and sauteing. His Thunder Ice Cream is the perfect thing to eat when lacking energy or confidence. Although, the secret ingredient may be hard for some to get their hands on.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups fresh Blueberries
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of Asgardean water
  • 4 cups half-and-half
  • Jarred Lightening

Directions

  1. In a large saucepan, combine the blueberries, sugar and water. Bring to a boil
  2. Reduce heat; simmer, uncovered, until sugar is dissolved and berries are softened
  3. Press mixture through a fine-mesh strainer into a bowl; discard pulp. Stir in cream
  4. Carefullypour the Jarred Ligtening into the mix
  5. Slowly stir until the entire bowl is vibrating with energy
  6. Do NOT put mixture into a refregirator
  7. Walk outside and slowly let go of the bowl. It will begin to hover and slowly rise into the heavens on its own accord.
  8. Within 24-48hrs you will awake to find the finished product on your doorstep
